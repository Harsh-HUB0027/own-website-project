    <h2>Internship Roadmap — India & Abroad</h2>
    <div class="two-col">
      <div>
        <h3>India — 6–12 month roadmap</h3>
        <ol>
          <li>Semester 4–6: Apply to industry internships (F&B service, kitchen, front office).</li>
          <li>Short courses: Food safety, Barista, Basic Hospitality skills.</li>
          <li>Local hotel chains and resorts — start with internship, build reference letters.</li>
          <li>Document experiences (photos, brief reports) to include in portfolio.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h3>Abroad — 6–18 month roadmap</h3>
        <ol>
          <li>Research countries: UAE, Singapore, Malaysia, UK — where hospitality internships are common.</li>
          <li>Apply via agencies or international internship portals; check visa rules.</li>
          <li>Consider paid placements after initial unpaid internships; network with alumni.</li>
          <li>Showcase language skills and adaptability — useful in multinational hotels.</li>
        </ol>
