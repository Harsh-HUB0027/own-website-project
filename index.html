Internship Roadmap — India & Abroad

India — 6–12 month roadmap

  1. Semester 4–6: Apply to industry internships (F&B service, kitchen, front office).
  2. Short courses: Food safety, Barista, Basic Hospitality skills.
  3. Local hotel chains and resorts — start with internship, build reference letters.
  4. Document experiences (photos, brief reports) to include in portfolio.

Abroad — 6–18 month roadmap

  1. Research countries: UAE, Singapore, Malaysia, UK — where hospitality internships are common.
  2. Apply via agencies or international internship portals; check visa rules.
  3. Consider paid placements after initial unpaid internships; network with alumni.
  4. Showcase language skills and adaptability — useful in multinational hotels.

Jobs — India & Abroad

Common entry-level roles: Front Office Executive, Food & Beverage Service Staff, Commis Chef, Housekeeping Associate, Trainee Manager. Mid-level roles include Assistant Manager, Food Production Supervisor, etc. Senior roles require experience and management skills.

India

Hotels, Resorts, Restaurant chains, Cruise lines, Event management companies.

Abroad

International hotels, hospitality groups, airlines (cabin crew/food service), luxury cruise liners.
